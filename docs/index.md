## Why Hestia feels different

<div class="home-grid">
  <article class="home-card">
    <h3>Exact weights</h3>
    <p>Every ingredient can be anchored to measurable units rather than fuzzy guesswork, which makes nutrition and cost calculations actually useful.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="home-card">
    <h3>One pantry model</h3>
    <p>The ingredient catalog acts as the single source of truth for pricing, aliases, and nutrition data across your whole cookbook.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="home-card">
    <h3>Readable by humans</h3>
    <p>Recipes stay editable in plain text, so you never lose the speed of a text editor or the audit trail of version control.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="home-card">
    <h3>Calm presentation</h3>
    <p>The browser UI and docs are meant to feel uplifting and useful, with enough polish to invite browsing but not enough noise to get in the way.</p>
  </article>
</div>
